Abstract

The integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) with value-based pricing (VBP) models is composed to redesign the landscape of healthcare market access and regulatory decision making. As health systems progressively shifting toward value-driven approaches, leveraging AI's analytical power offers new opportunities to optimize pricing strategies based on real-world outcomes and patient-centric system of measurement. The integration supports more accurate and transparent evaluations of value, enabling the development of systems that are both flexible and based on real-world evidence. Furthermore, AI can streamline regulatory pathways by enabling predictive modelling, risk stratification, and data-driven scenario analysis, thereby accelerating time-to-market for innovative therapies. This model shift holds significant potential to align stakeholder—including clients, regulators, providers, and patients—around outcomes-focused care delivery, while also addressing cost-effectiveness and impartiality considerations. Ultimately, the junction of AI and VBP can drive more sustainable healthcare systems and foster access to high-value medical innovations globally.
